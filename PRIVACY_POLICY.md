# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 6, 2026**

## Overview

LLMinx Solver ("the App") is a Megaminx last layer solving utility. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

## Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically, the App:

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not make network requests
- Does not access the internet
- Does not share any data with third parties

## Local Storage

The App stores user preferences (such as theme settings) locally on your device using Android's DataStore. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing App data

## Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

- **Storage access**: Only used to read wallpaper images for theming purposes. No data is uploaded or shared.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
